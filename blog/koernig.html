Web Design: Körnig

Design Implementation for local Organic Supermarket "Körnig"

Introduction

In today's competitive market, having a strong online presence is vital for businesses to thrive. The Körnig Organic Supermarket recognized this need and sought to create a website that would not only showcase their brand values and product offerings but also provide an engaging and seamless experience for users. This project report outlines the process of designing and implementing the Körnig Organic Supermarket website, focusing on the challenges faced, the tools used, and the successful results achieved.

The project's goal was to develop and implement an engaging design for the Körnig Organic Supermarket website, which functions on various devices and visually presents the brand. Figma was used for the design, while HTML, CSS, and JavaScript were employed for implementation. Despite challenges, an aesthetically appealing, user-friendly, and search engine-optimized website was created.

Software and tools

The design process involved analyzing user needs, creating a user-friendly interface, and testing the design with real users. The app design featured features like plant and seed selection, soil preparation, and watering and fertilizing reminders. The use of Figma allowed efficient prototyping and testing of the app design.

Conclusion

Overall, the design process for the Körnig Organic Supermarket website was a rewarding and successful endeavor. Utilizing Figma as the design tool proved to be highly effective, allowing for the creation of a visually appealing, user-friendly website. By overcoming the challenges of learning Figma and adhering to interface guidelines, the final result is a captivating online presence that showcases the Körnig brand and offers an engaging experience for users across various platforms. This project demonstrates the importance of a well-designed website in today's digital landscape and the impact it can have on a business's success.
